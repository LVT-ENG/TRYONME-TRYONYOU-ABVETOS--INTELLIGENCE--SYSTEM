## ğŸ“¦ Adding Your Assets

**IMPORTANT:** Before deploying, add your real assets to `/public/`:

1. **Images**: Place in `/public/assets/images/`
   - Wardrobe items (clothing images)
   - Showroom looks
   - Glow-up before/after photos
