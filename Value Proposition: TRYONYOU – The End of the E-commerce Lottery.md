**TRYONYOU – ABVETOS – ULTRA – PLUS – ULTIMATUM** is a comprehensive "Emotional Fashion Intelligence" ecosystem protected by international patent (**PCT/EP2025/067317**). It is not just a virtual try-on; it is a **closed-loop platform** that unifies the shopping experience from biometric scanning and emotional recommendation to automated on-demand production and secure biometric payment. It functions as a digital extension of physical stores, transforming e-commerce into a "Digital Atelier".

## The Problem: The "E-commerce Lottery"

The fashion industry suffers from a **30-40%** return rate due to uncertainty in sizing (S, M, L are inaccurate) and a lack of emotional connection, leading to millions in losses and a massive environmental impact (textile waste and CO₂).

## The Solution: Mathematical Certainty and Zero Waste

Tryonyou's purpose is to eliminate uncertainty and waste. Its mission is to move from "guessing sizes" to offering **"Mathematical Certainty"**. It aims to reduce the return rate to zero and foster a **"Zero Stock, Zero Waste"** industry through *Just-in-Time* production.

## Innovative Features: The 4 Engines

The system is based on an architecture of 4 interconnected engines:

1.  **Parametric 3D Avatar (Fit Engine):** Uses biometric scanning (via *Gemini Pro Vision* and mobile cameras) to create a digital twin with **millimeter precision (99.7%)**. It does not use standard sizes; it simulates the real physics of the fabric on the digital body.

    ![3D Digital Avatar](https://i.imgur.com/j3d2ud8lTo5m.jpeg)

2.  **PAU (Personal Avatar Unit):** An Emotional Artificial Intelligence (represented by a White Peacock, Agent 001). Unlike cold algorithms, PAU analyzes the **emotional context** (e.g., "How do you feel today?", "Is it for a gala or work?") to recommend garments that connect with the user's mood.

3.  **CAP (Creative Auto-Production):** If the perfect garment does not exist in stock, this module automatically generates the industrial files (DXF patterns) and sends them to the smart factory (*LiveIt Factory*) for immediate production, eliminating excess stock.

4.  **ABVET (Biometric Payment):** A high-security payment system that uses **dual biometrics (Iris + Voice)**. It eliminates the friction of traditional checkout and ensures the traceability of the transaction on the Blockchain (ADBETOS).
