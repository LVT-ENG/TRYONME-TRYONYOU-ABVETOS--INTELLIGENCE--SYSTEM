# TryOnYou Ultra V9.0 Release Notes

## Google Platform Integrations

### Gemini 2.0 Integration
We are excited to announce the integration of **Google Gemini 2.0 Flash** into the TryOnYou recommendation engine. This upgrade significantly enhances the speed and accuracy of our AI stylist, providing real-time, personalized fashion advice with response times under 500ms.

### Agent 70
The core logic is now powered by **Agent 70**, our advanced orchestration agent that leverages Gemini 2.0 to understand user mood, body type, and inventory context to deliver hyper-personalized recommendations.

### MediaPipe
Privacy-first biometric processing is now handled entirely client-side using **Google MediaPipe**, ensuring that your data never leaves your device while providing precise body measurements.
